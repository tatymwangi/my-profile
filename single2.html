About Tatiana

Family

Familes come in different forms. You may have many members or just a few. But the most important things in the family are love, peace and unity. Other things may change us, but we start and end with the family. No matter where you go, they will always be there. Being a family means you are a part of something very wonderful. It means you will love and be loved for the rest of your life. And family ties mean that no matter how much you might want to run from your family, you can’t.Think of your family today and every day thereafter, don’t let the busy world of today keep you from showing how much you love and appreciate your family. Families aren’t always blood. It’s the people in your life who want you in theirs; the ones who accept you for who you are. The ones that would do anything to see you smile and who love you no matter what. Family is not an important thing. It’s everything.

Friendship

A friend is one of the best things you can be and the greatest things you can have. They are the people in your life who make you laugh louder, smile brighter and live better. A true friend is someone who thinks that you are a good egg even though he knows that you are slightly cracked. Friendship is the only cement that will ever hold the world together. Truly great friends are hard to find, difficult to leave, and impossible to forget.
